    Job Description

    • Preparing and analyzing financial statements, budgets, forecasts, and cash flow projections
    • Ensuring compliance with accounting standards, tax regulations, and internal policies
    • Managing financial risks and controls, and implementing best practices
    • Supervising and mentoring the finance staff and ensuring timely and accurate financial reporting
    • Liaising with external auditors, bankers, investors, and other stakeholders

    Requirement

    • A bachelor's degree in accounting, finance, or related field
    • A professional certification such as CPA, CMA, or ACCA
    • At least five years of experience in a similar role, preferably in a large or multinational organization
    • Strong knowledge of accounting principles, financial analysis, budgeting, and forecasting
    • Proficiency in MS Excel and accounting software
    • Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills
